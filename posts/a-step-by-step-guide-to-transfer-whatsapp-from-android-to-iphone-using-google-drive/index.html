Are you switching your phone from Android to iPhone? Don’t worry, you don’t have to lose your WhatsApp messages in the process. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to transfer your WhatsApp messages from Android to iPhone using Google Drive. Switching from Android to an iPhone? Congratulations! You’re in for a treat. But having to leave all of your WhatsApp chats behind? That’s a bummer. Don’t worry though, you don’t have to say goodbye to all of your WhatsApp conversations forever. Thanks to Google Drive, you can easily transfer WhatsApp from your old Android to your new iPhone. And with this step-by-step guide, it’s easier than ever! Here’s how:

  1. On your Android device, open WhatsApp and go to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup.

  2. Select the “Back Up” option to back up your chats and media to Google Drive.

  3. Next, download and install WhatsApp on your iPhone.

  4. Then, sign into the same Google account used to back up your chats on Android.

  5. Now, a prompt will appear asking if you’d like to restore your chats from Google Drive. Select “Restore” and voila! All of your WhatsApp conversations are right where you left them.

Now that you’ve transferred WhatsApp from Android to iPhone, you can start messaging with your friends without missing a beat. Enjoy your new iPhone!

Now, using the same Google account, log into the Apple device and go to App Store to download and install WhatsApp. Open the app and verify your mobile number. Finally, you should delete the WhatsApp back up from your Android device. This can be done by going to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup and tapping on ‘Delete Backup’. This will ensure that the data is not stored on two devices. After this, the transfer process should be complete.

3. Restore WhatsApp from Backup to iPhone#

If you have changed to a new iPhone and need to transfer your WhatsApp data, this step-by-step guide will make sure your important messages, photos, and videos are all settled in quickly with minimal effort. First, you need to create a Google Drive backup of your current Android device. Then, you can easily restore it into your new iPhone. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Open your WhatsApp app on your Android device and click the three vertical dots in the top right corner. Select Settings and choose Chats.
  2. Under Chats, select Chat backup. Press Back up to begin the process. Wait until the backup process is complete.
  3. Download the Google Drive app onto your iPhone and sign in with the same Google Account credentials that you used to back up your WhatsApp on Android.
  4. Once in the Google Drive app, expand the menu in the top left and select Backups. You will then be able to see your WhatsApp backup. Finally, press restore to have all of your WhatsApp data transferred to your new iPhone.
