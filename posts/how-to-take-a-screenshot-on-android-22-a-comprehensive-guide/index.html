If you are still having issues with screenshots, you can try clearing the cache and data on your device. To do so, go to ‘Settings’, then the ‘Apps’ menu, and select ‘Screenshot’. Select ‘Clear Cache’ to clear the stored data. Then, select ‘Clear Data’ and restart your device.

You can also try resetting your device. To do this, go to ‘Settings’, then ‘Backup and Reset’, and select ‘Reset Phone’. This will reset the phone to its original settings, and you should be able to take a screenshot without any issues.
