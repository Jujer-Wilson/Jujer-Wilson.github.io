Step One: Plug your Android TV Box into your TV.

The first and most important step you need to do before anything else is to plug your Android TV Box into your TV. All you need is a HDMI cable to do this. Connect one end of the HDMI cable to the HDMI port on your TV and the other end to the HDMI port on the Android TV Box.

Step Two: Enable Screen Mirroring on your Android TV Box.

Once you have plugged in your Android TV Box, you will need to enable the screen mirroring feature. To do this, go to Settings, and choose Display & Sounds from the menu. Then, select Enable Screen Mirroring from the sub-menu.

Step Three: Activate Screen Mirroring on your Android Device.

Once you have enabled screen mirroring on your Android TV Box, you will need to activate the same feature on your Android device. To do this, go to Settings, and choose Display & Sounds from the menu. Then, select Activate Screen Mirroring from the sub-menu.

Step Four: Select your Android TV Box.

After you have activated screen mirroring on your Android device, you will need to select your Android TV Box from the list of available devices. Select your device from the list and you should then see your device name appear on the Android TV Box.

Step Five: That’s it!

Once you have selected your Android TV Box from the list of available devices, you are ready to start screen mirroring. You should now be able to see your Android phone or tablet screen on your Android TV Box. Now you can watch YouTube videos or share pictures on a big screen with your friends.
