1.What is a QR Code#

A QR Code, or Quick Response Code, is a two dimensional barcode that is used to store information. They are commonly used in many industries including marketing and logistics to quickly access product and customer data. With the increasing popularity of smartphones, QR Codes have become a quick and convenient way to access websites and other digital content. In this guide, we will walk you through how to scan QR Codes on your Android phone.

Firstly, you need to download and install a QR Code scanner. There are many free apps available in the Play Store, so be sure to choose one that is reliable. Once installed, launch the app and grant it permission to use the camera on your device.

Next, point your camera at the QR Code. The QR scanner should automatically detect the code and display a message. Tap on the message to view the content or access the link.

Once you are done, tap the back button to return to the scanner, or exit the app. This will ensure that the scanner is ready to scan the next QR Code.

By following the steps above, you can now scan QR Codes quickly and easily with your Android phone.
