First, go to your phone’s settings and select the Accessibility option. Then, find the option called TalkBack and switch it off. Once it’s off, you’ll see a prompt asking you to accept the changes. Simply accept the changes and you’re all set.

Next, go to the Lock Screen settings and select the ‘None’ option. This will disable any gestures and active elements while the phone is locked.

Finally, restart your phone to ensure that the changes have been properly implemented. Restarting the phone is the only way to make sure that the changes you’ve made are permanent.

With just a few short steps, you can disable TalkBack on your Android lock screen and take back your freedom.
