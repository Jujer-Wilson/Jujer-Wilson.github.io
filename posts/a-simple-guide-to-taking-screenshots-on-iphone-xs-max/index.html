Alternatively, the AssistiveTouch feature allows you to take screenshots with just one hand. To enable this feature, go to Settings > General > Accessibility and toggle on AssistiveTouch. You can now access AssistiveTouch through the AssistiveTouch icon that appears on the iPhone XS Max’s display. Now tap Device > More > Screenshot. This will trigger the screenshot effect and save the photo to the Photos app.
