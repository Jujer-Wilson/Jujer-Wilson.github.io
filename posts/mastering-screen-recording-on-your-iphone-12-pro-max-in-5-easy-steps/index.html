Screen recording on your iPhone 12 Pro Max can be a great way to capture content for social media posts, creating screencasts, or even taking notes. This article aims to show you how to screen record on your iPhone 12 Pro Max in 5 easy steps.

The first step is to access Control Centre on your device. Swipe up from the bottom of your home screen or swipe down from the top corners of the screen. Look for the screen recording icon which is a circle with a dot in the centre.
