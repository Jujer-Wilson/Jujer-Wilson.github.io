1. What are Cache and Cookies#

Android tablets are convenient devices for both work and play. They are also a great way to stay connected. If you’re having trouble with your Android tablet, you may want to try clearing the cache and cookies. But what exactly are cache and cookies?

  2. Scroll through the list of apps and select the web browser you use.
  3. Tap Storage and select Clear Cache first, then Clear Data.
  4. Return to the list of apps and select the app you want to clear.
  5. Tap Clear Cache and then Clear Data.

2. Clearing Cache and Cookies on Android Tablet#

Cache and cookies are files stored on your Android tablet by your web browser. To double-check that the data has been deleted, go back to Settings and select Privacy. This will show the size of your cache files. If the size is now 0, this means that the cache and cookies have been cleared.
