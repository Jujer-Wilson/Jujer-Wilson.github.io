I. How to Take Screenshot on Macbook Air M2#

Taking screenshots on Macbook Air M2 is a great way to capture and share important content on your laptop. The good news is, taking a screenshot isn’t complicated and you can easily learn how to do it with this step-by-step guide. First, press Command-Shift-3 on your keyboard. This will take a full-screen screenshot of your Mac. Alternatively, you can press Command-Shift-4 to turn the cursor into a reticle which allows you to select a specific part of the screen for your screenshot. After that, your Mac should automatically save the screenshot to your Desktop as a .png file. You can also use the Command-Control-Shift-3 shortcut to take a screenshot without saving it immediately. Finally, if you need to take a screenshot of a menu or a drop-down menu, press Command-Shift-4 and then press the Spacebar; your cursor will turn into a camera icon which you can then hover over the menu. And that’s it - you’ve successfully taken a screenshot on your Macbook Air M2!
