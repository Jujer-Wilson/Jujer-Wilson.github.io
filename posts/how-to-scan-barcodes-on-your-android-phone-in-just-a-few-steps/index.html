Scanning barcodes with your Android phone is a great way to save time and maximize accuracy. It’s important to follow security measures while doing so, however. Here are a few tips to ensure your barcodes are scanned securely:

  2. Be wary of codes that appear to be too good to be true. If a code has too many numbers, symbols, or letters, it could be a sign that it has been tampered with.

  3. Always keep your phone updated with the latest security patches. Keeping your device up-to-date helps protect you against hacking or tampering.
