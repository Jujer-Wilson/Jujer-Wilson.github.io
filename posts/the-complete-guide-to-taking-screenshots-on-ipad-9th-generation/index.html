Q.1 How do I take a screenshot on iPad 9th Generation?

A. Taking a screenshot on iPad 9th Generation is easy. Just press the Top button and the Volume up button at the same time. You should hear a camera shutter sound, and the screenshot will appear in the bottom left corner of your screen.

Q.2 How do I view screenshots I’ve taken on my iPad 9th Generation?

A. To view screenshots you’ve taken on your iPad 9th Generation, open the Photos app. Tap on the Albums tab, and you’ll see a folder called Screenshots. You can find all of your screenshots here.

Q.3 How do I share screenshots I’ve taken on my iPad 9th Generation?

A. To share screenshots you’ve taken on your iPad 9th Generation, open the Photos app. Tap on the Albums tab, and you’ll see a folder called Screenshots. You can select the screenshot you want to share, and then tap the share button in the bottom left corner. From here, you can select how you’d like to share the screenshot.

Q.4 How do I take a screenshot of an app window on my iPad 9th Generation?

A. To take a screenshot of an app window on your iPad 9th Generation, press and hold the Top button and the Home button at the same time. You should hear a camera shutter sound, and the screenshot will appear in the bottom left corner of your screen.

Q.5 How do I capture a portion of my iPad 9th Generation’s screen?

A. To capture a portion of your iPad 9th Generation’s screen, press and hold the Top button and the Volume up button at the same time. You should see a small box that you can use to select which portion of the screen you want to capture. You can move and resize the box by dragging the edges. Once you have the area you’d like to capture, release the buttons and you should hear a camera shutter sound. The screenshot will appear in the bottom left corner of your screen.
