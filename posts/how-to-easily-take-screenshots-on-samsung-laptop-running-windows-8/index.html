1. What is a Screenshot#

A screenshot is an image of your computer screen taken at a particular moment. It’s helpful to capture a moment or image on your screen to share with others. Taking screenshots on a Samsung laptop running Windows 8 is easy. All you need to do is press certain key combinations to activate the built-in screenshot utility. Here is a guide on how to take screenshots on your Samsung laptop with Windows 8.

First, press the Windows key and Prt Sc (Print Screen) key at the same time. Doing so will capture the entire screen and save it in the Screenshots folder in Pictures. Alternatively, you can press Alt and Prt Sc at the same time to capture only the active window or program.

You can also take a partial screenshot using the Snipping Tool. Go to the Start menu, type in Snipping Tool, and open the application. Then, click New and drag the mouse along the area you want to capture. Finally, release the mouse button and your snippet will be saved in the Pictures folder.

Finally, if you want to take multiple screenshots quickly, you can use the Screen capture hotkey. Press and hold the Windows Key and press Prt Sc multiple times. Each screenshot will be saved in the Screenshots folder.

Taking screenshots on a Samsung laptop running Windows 8 is quick and easy. So you don’t have to worry about any complicated setup or software downloads. Just follow the steps outlined above and you’ll be capturing screenshots in no time.
