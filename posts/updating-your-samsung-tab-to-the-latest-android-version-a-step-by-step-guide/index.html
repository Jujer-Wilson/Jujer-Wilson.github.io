1. Preparation#

Updating your Samsung Tab to the latest Android version can be a daunting undertaking. Before getting started, be sure to back up all your data. I. Check Device Requirement#

To update Android version on a Samsung Tab, preparation is essential. To begin with, make sure your device is connected to a stable Wi-Fi connection and has a fully charged battery. Afterwards, you must go to the Settings menu > Software Update and check for any new available updates. If the update is available, select Download Now. After downloading, tap Install. Finally, wait for the installation to finish and restart your device. Following these steps, you can easily upgrade your Android version in a Samsung Tab.
