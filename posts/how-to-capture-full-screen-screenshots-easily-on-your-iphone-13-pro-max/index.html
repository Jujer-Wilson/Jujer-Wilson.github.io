1. Setting up iPhone 13 Pro Max for Screenshots#

Capturing full-screen screenshots on your iPhone 13 Pro Max is easy and can be done in a few simple steps. Firstly, go to the ‘Settings’ app on your device and tap on the ‘General’ option. Next, scroll down until you reach ‘Accessibility’ and select it. Once you’re in the Accessibility menu, select ‘Guided Access’ and turn the feature on by tapping the toggle switch. After that, open the app or page that you want to take a screenshot of and triple-click the side button of your device. A confirmation alert will appear, tap ‘Start’ and you will be able to take full-screen screenshots.
