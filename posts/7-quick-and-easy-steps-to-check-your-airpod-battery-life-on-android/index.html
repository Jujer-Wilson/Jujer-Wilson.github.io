2. Steps to Check AirPod Battery Life on Android#

It can be complicated to keep track of your AirPod battery life when using an Android. Fortunately, with a few basic steps, you can easily check your AirPod battery life on Android. Here are seven quick and easy steps to help you out:

  1. Open the AirPods case and place your AirPods in your ears.

  2. Go to the Bluetooth settings on your Android device and select AirPods.

  3. Select the battery icon next to the AirPods name under Bluetooth settings.

  4. A pop-up window will appear showing the battery percentage for each AirPod.

  5. To check the battery percentage for both AirPods, tap on the AirPods name.

  6. A detailed battery screen will appear showing the battery life for both AirPods.

  7. To close the battery screen, simply tap the back button and you’re good to go.
