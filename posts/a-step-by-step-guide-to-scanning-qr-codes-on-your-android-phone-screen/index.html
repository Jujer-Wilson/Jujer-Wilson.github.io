1. Overview of QR Codes#

QR Codes are two-dimensional barcodes that are used to store large amounts of data in a small space. By scanning a QR Code, the user’s device can access a website, text message, or application. In this step by step guide, we will show you how to scan QR Codes on your Android phone screen.

First, you need to have a QR Code scanner installed on your phone. You can easily find and download a scanner from the Google Play store. Once your scanner is installed, open it and point your camera at the QR Code you want to scan. The scanner should recognize and read the code almost immediately.

Once you’ve scanned the code, you will be presented with an action. Depending on the type of code you scanned, you may receive a link, an application, or text message. If it’s a link, you can open it in the browser or with another app on your device.

Lastly, if you have an error scanning the code, make sure the code is facing the right way. QR Codes can only be scanned correctly when the entire code is visible on your screen. Additionally, if the code is too small, try zooming in or using a fullscreen scanner.

Scanning QR Codes on your Android phone screen is an easy process. With the help of an app, you can instantly access text messages, websites, and applications without typing a single word.
