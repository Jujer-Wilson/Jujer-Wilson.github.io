1. How to Delete Voice Notes on WhatsApp Android#

Deleting voice notes from WhatsApp on Android is easy and quick. To do this, simply open the chat that contains the voice note you wish to delete. Tap and hold on the voice note until a menu appears. In the menu, tap the trash can icon to delete the voice note. You’ll be asked to confirm the delete selection before it’s permanently removed. Once you confirm the delete, the voice note will be removed from the chat.

It’s worth noting that you can also delete voice notes from the main WhatsApp window. To do this, open the main WhatsApp window and select the Documents tab. Here, you’ll see a list of all the voice notes currently stored in your WhatsApp chats. Tap and hold on the one you wish to delete, then tap the delete button. Once again, you’ll be asked to confirm the delete before the voice note is permanently removed.

Deleting voice notes from WhatsApp can help keep your conversations organized and free up storage space on your device. It’s a simple process that can be done in a few steps.
