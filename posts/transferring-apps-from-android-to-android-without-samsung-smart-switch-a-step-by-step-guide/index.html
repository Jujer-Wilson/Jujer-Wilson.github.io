1. Introduction to Smart Switch#

Smart Switch by Samsung allows you to easily transfer your data and apps from your old Android device to a brand new or refurbished Samsung device. Through this quick and easy-to-use application, you can transfer your Apps, contacts, music, photos, calendar, texts and even more with just a few simple taps. Whether you lost your device, got a new one, or just want to transfer between two Android devices, this app is a great way to ensure you don’t lose any of your important data. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to transfer apps from Android to Android without Samsung Smart Switch.

First, you’ll need to download the app onto both the source and target devices. Open the Google Play Store on both devices and search for Samsung Smart Switch. Once you have found the app, tap the Install button. Once the installation is complete, open the app and accept the Terms and Conditions. On the source device, select the Sender option. On the target device, select the Receiver option.

Next, you’ll need to set up a connection between the two devices. This is done by establishing a direct Wi-Fi connection between the source and target devices. Once the two devices are connected, you can select which Apps you want to transfer. Select the checkbox next to each App and tap the Send button on the source device. On the target device, you’ll see a list of Apps with an Install button. Tap the Install button and your Apps will be transferred to the target device.

Finally, once all of the Apps have been successfully transferred, you can disconnect the two devices. All of your Apps from the source device have been successfully transferred to the target device. You can delete the Samsung Smart Switch app if you want, or you can keep it for future transfers.

Using Samsung Smart Switch, you can easily transfer your Apps from one Android device to another. Follow the steps outlined above and you’ll be able to quickly and easily transfer your Apps without any hassle.
