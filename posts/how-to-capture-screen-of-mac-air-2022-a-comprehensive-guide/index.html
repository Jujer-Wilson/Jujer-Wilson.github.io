1. What Is A Screenshot?#

A screenshot is an image taken of what is showing on your computer, phone, or tablet screen. It is used to record the activity currently displayed on the screen, which can be helpful for tutorials, documentation, and troubleshooting. In this guide, we will explore how to capture the screen of the new Mac Air 2022.

To take a screenshot on Mac Air 2022, open the ‘Command’ menu and press ‘Shift + Command+3’ simultaneously. This will take a full-screen screenshot and save it directly to your desktop. Alternatively, you can press ‘Shift + Command+4’ to select a portion of the screen to capture.

Another way to take a screenshot is using the Preview tool. To open Preview, press ‘Command+Space’ and type Preview. Once opened, click on ‘File’ and select ‘Take Screenshot’. You can then choose the type of screenshot you wish to take.

When taking screenshots on Mac Air 2022, you can also use the Screenshot app. This app allows you to record the whole or part of the screen of your Mac Air 2022 and annotate it as needed. To open the app, press ‘Command+Space’ and type ‘Screenshot’.

In conclusion, using the different options available, you can easily capture the screen of your Mac Air 2022. Whether you take a full-screen screenshot or just part of the screen, you can use the different tools available to capture the image you need.
