1. Preparing to Connect Your iPhone and Android#

To transfer contacts from an iPhone to an Android device wirelessly requires a few steps. First, connect both devices to the same Wi-Fi network. On the iPhone, go to Settings, then select the iCloud option and turn on the “Contacts” option. Next, open the Contacts app on the Android device, then select the “Menu” button in the top right corner. Select “Transfer Contacts” and then “Receive via Wi-Fi”. Once selected, both devices will be ready to transfer the contacts. Finally, open the Contacts app on the iPhone and select the “Share” button. Select the contacts to transfer then select “Send via Wi-fi”. The contacts will then transfer to the Android device.

To ensure that the contacts are successfully transferred, open the Contacts app on the Android device and select “Transfer Contacts”. This will display a list of all contacts transferred from the iPhone. If you need to transfer more contacts from your iPhone, simply repeat the steps above. It is important to remember that you must stay connected to the same Wi-Fi network for the entire process.

If you want to transfer contacts from your Android device to your iPhone, the steps are similar. First, connect both devices to the same Wi-Fi network. On the Android device, select the “Contacts” option from the main menu. Select the contacts you want to transfer and select the “Share” button. Select the “Send via Wi-Fi” option, which will transfer the contacts to the iPhone.

Finally, open the Contacts app on the iPhone and select the “Import” button. Select the “Import from Android” option and the contacts will transfer over. By following these steps, you can easily transfer contacts from your iPhone to your Android device or from Android to iPhone wirelessly.

Transferring contacts from an iPhone to an Android device can be done easily and wirelessly with just a few taps. Start by downloading the free Gmail application from your Android device’s app store. Once the app is downloaded, open the app and sign in with your Google account. To transfer contacts from your iPhone, open the Settings of your iPhone, choose the Mail, Contacts, Calendar option, and then select Add Account.
