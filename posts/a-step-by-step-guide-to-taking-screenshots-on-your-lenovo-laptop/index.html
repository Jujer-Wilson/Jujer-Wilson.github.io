Q1: What is a screenshot?

A1: A screenshot is an image of the current display on your computer or device. It captures what’s on the screen, including any menus, icons, windows, and other visual content.

Q2: How do I take a screenshot on my Lenovo laptop?

A2: Taking a screenshot on a Lenovo laptop is simple and easy. All you need to do is press the ‘Print Screen’ button on your laptop keyboard and the current display will be captured in image form.

Q3: How do I save the screenshot?

A3: Once you have taken the screenshot, you can save the image by pressing the Windows logo key + the ‘S’ key. You can also press the ‘Ctrl’ + ‘S’ keys to save the screenshot.

Q4: How do I find my saved screenshots?

A4: By default, screenshots are saved in the Pictures folder. To access the saved screenshots, open the File Explorer, click on ‘Pictures’ in the left pane, and you will find the saved screenshots in the right pane.

Q5: What file type are my screenshots saved as?

A5: By default, screenshots are saved as PNG files. You can also change the file type to JPG or BMP by pressing the ‘Ctrl’ + ‘Shift’ + ‘S’ keys after capturing the screenshot.
